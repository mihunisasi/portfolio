Michael Hutahaean is an incoming Master–Doctoral student in Economics at SOAS University of London and currently serves as Deputy Programme Manager at [Resilience Development Initiative (RDI)](https://rdiglobal.org/), a global think tank with a strong focus on conducting studies related to resilience and sustainable development.

He supports business development and strategic partnerships at RDI, while also contributing to a diverse portfolio of research projects and consultancies supported by prominent funders and organizations, including the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP), the Wageningen Centre for Development Innovation (WCDI), the Urban Studies Foundation (USF), Deloitte, and Ernst & Young (EY), among others. His expertise and interests align with his commitment to fostering sustainable practices and innovative solutions in the built environment.

**Research Focus**: sustainable urban planning, megaproject development, circular economy, economic and development policy, climate change adaptation, and sustainable and resilient infrastructure.
